Our Journey

Super Bazaar has been the heartbeat of South Asian culture in Buffalo since 1990. Started by brothers Taraq Khan and Ashaq Khan, our store began as a place for community connection and South Asian staples. From offering rides to students on weekends to delivering groceries during the pandemic, our mission has always been care, culture, and community.
